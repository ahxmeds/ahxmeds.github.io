About

A concise academic homepage with your research at the center.

I am a researcher working on deep learning for generative modeling, inverse problems, and multiscale techniques. My interests include flow matching, score-based modeling, generative priors, uncertainty-aware methods, and scalable architectures for imaging.

I am especially interested in methods that connect elegant mathematical ideas with real-world scientific impact, including medical imaging, geophysics, and physically-informed learning.

Research

Core research themes

Generative Modeling

Flow matching, score-based models, latent generative priors, and efficient training methods for high-dimensional scientific data.

Inverse Problems

Learning-based approaches for reconstructing hidden structure from indirect, noisy, and incomplete measurements.

Scientific AI

Machine learning systems tailored to medical imaging, geophysical inversion, and physically motivated data regimes.
